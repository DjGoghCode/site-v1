Dear Yvonne,

Dear Yvonne,In 1996, my mentor, Moe Ross, was sitting with cultural anthropologist, Angeles Arrien, as she shared her dreams to start a legacy book company. After walking the earth for 49 years, Moe felt a deep calling to participate in a paradigm shift in business and storytelling. In response, Angeles gave Moe the following prayer, “May the art and craft of the first half of my life come together so I can add more love in the world”. 27 years later, Moe has written over 128 books for families by weaving the golden thread from one generation to the next—capturing what they have come to know.

I share this story with you because, no matter how the path emerges ahead of us, I am honored to witness this powerful, pivotal evolution you are participating in. In our first conversation, you said, “There is something that connects us... No matter how different we are, there is something that unites us all.” It occurs to me we are living in a time where it has never been more important to amplify this principle—to feel, to connect, to love, to unite.

We are over the moon about meeting you. Getting to help you create expansive, felt, and visual forms to communicate what matters most would be a great joy and privilege. Here’s to the people and stories, whose hands, make our days infinitely more beautiful—with their art and craft.


Thank you so much.
With Gratitude & Respect,
Chloe & Dj
